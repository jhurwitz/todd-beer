Todd's Memorial Beer Raffle

Recently a great member of the Bay Area beer community passed away from an unexpected heart attack. Todd Riehl was only 47 years old. He was an astoundingly kind and generous friend, a fanatic lover of music, and a scientist devoted to helping find new drugs to fight cancer. He leaves behind a wife, Jess, and two young children, Zoe and Izaak.

Todd was a huge fan of craft beer, and he would have wanted his beers to go to people who would appreciate them. We have sorted through the 1500+ bottles that were in his cellar and created prize packages that showcase the breweries and styles that Todd loved most. All donations will go directly to Todd’s family.

Raffle Details

  • For each $10 donated you receive one raffle “Ticket” towards the prize drawing, which we expect to take place on October 9. (For example, if you donate $100, you receive 10 tickets.)
  • Each ticket gives you a chance to win all of the available prizes. Individuals are eligible to win multiple prizes if they have bought multiple tickets.
  • We have over 100 prize packages. The first 50 prizes are available right now, and we will add more once we reach our first goal of $10,000 raised for the family.

    • Details on how to enter are coming soon!
